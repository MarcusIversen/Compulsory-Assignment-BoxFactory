Compulsory Assignment - Box Factory

Business Case The local box factory needs to keep track of their products. The manager wants to be able to see all the magnificent boxes on a dedicated web application while he's away for golfing. He has just called you up, and now it's your job. It should be possible to see a brief overview of many different products, elaborate details of a single box, update a box and delete a box.
